Fernet con Coca

Fernet con Coca is an iconic Argentinian cocktail made by combining the bitter herbal liqueur Fernet with cola. Known for its bold taste and cultural significance, it's especially popular in CÃ³rdoba and at gatherings with friends.

It's quick to prepare, requires just two ingredients, and can be adjusted in strength to match your personal taste. Serve it cold and enjoy it responsibly!

Ingredients

  • Fernet (Branca preferred)
  • Cola (Coca-Cola recommended)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: Lemon slice for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with plenty of ice.
  2. Pour in Fernet, typically 30% of the glass. You can adjust to taste.
  3. Top up the rest of the glass with cola.
  4. Stir gently to mix.
  5. Add a lemon slice if desired and serve immediately.
